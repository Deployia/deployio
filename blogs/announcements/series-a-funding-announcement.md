# Deployio Raises $25M Series A to Democratize Cloud Deployments

_Published on December 19, 2024 by the Deployio Leadership Team_

**SAN FRANCISCO, CA** – Deployio, the leading cloud deployment platform that simplifies application deployment for developers and enterprises, today announced it has raised $25 million in Series A funding. The round was led by Bessemer Venture Partners, with participation from existing investors First Round Capital and Founder Collective, along with new investors including prominent angels from GitHub, Docker, and Kubernetes.

This funding will accelerate Deployio's mission to democratize cloud deployments, making enterprise-grade deployment capabilities accessible to developers and teams of all sizes.

## Addressing the Deployment Complexity Crisis

Modern application deployment has become increasingly complex, with developers spending up to 40% of their time on deployment-related tasks instead of building features. According to recent industry surveys, 73% of engineering teams report that deployment complexity is their biggest productivity bottleneck.

_"We founded Deployio because we experienced this pain firsthand,"_ said Alex Chen, CEO and Co-founder of Deployio. _"As engineers at companies like Uber and Stripe, we saw talented developers struggling with deployment pipelines instead of focusing on what they do best – writing code. We knew there had to be a better way."_
